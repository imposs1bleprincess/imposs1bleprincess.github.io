Artist's Statement

The foundation of my art & design serve as a function of understanding myself & the world & how things function within the world. My curiosity lies within the intangible & the things the pass every day, but never care to notice. Creative technology is a major component of the media I produce & has been useful for me to unveil the artistic design of my curiousities. The media I create often involves distortive mirroring techniques that uncover realms exhibiting symmetrical patterns, arching light patterns, & alien-like biota wuth recursive, colurful expression.
